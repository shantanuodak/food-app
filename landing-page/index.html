FAQ

Common questions

Is this a calorie-only app?

No. Food App tracks calories plus protein, carbs, and fats per entry and daily total.

How accurate are the estimates?

Each log includes confidence and assumptions, and you can edit items before saving. Deterministic parsing is used first, with AI fallback only when needed.

Will photo logging be supported?

Not in the MVP. The backend is intentionally designed to support photo and menu parsing in later releases.

Is Food App available now?

Food App is currently in MVP build phase. Join the waitlist for early access updates.
